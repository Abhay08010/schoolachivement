School Achivments

President AWWA Rolling Trophy Ethics Bowl 2022
Won first prize in President AWWA Rolling Trophy Ethics Bowl 2022.

The school hosted the second season of President AWWA, Rolling Trophy, Ethics Bowl- 2022. The event is held annually under the aegis of AWWA, a non profit organisation for the welfare of dependents of army personnel. It seeks to foster intellectual conviction and strong moral bearing in the future generations.



AWES English Debate
Won first prize in AWES English Debate

Army Public School, Delhi Cantt created history when its star debaters won the trophy at the AWES National English Debate Competition. It was a long and exciting journey for the team APS DC to reach this pinnacle of success.
